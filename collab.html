CALLING ALL THE LOCAL THRIFT STORES!!

Join us in revolutionizing the fashion industry with sustainability and style.



Why Collaborate with Us?

  • Boost Your Visibility: Gain exposure to our vibrant community of fashion-forward Gen Z shoppers who are always on the lookout for unique thrifted treasures.
  • Promote Sustainability: Together, we can champion sustainable fashion practices, reducing waste and supporting ethical consumption.
  • Empower Local Businesses: As a local thrift store, you play a crucial role in our mission to create a diverse, dynamic marketplace that celebrates individuality and supports small businesses.

Instructions for Accepting Clothes

To ensure we provide our customers with high-quality, stylish, and sustainable pieces, we have set some guidelines for accepting clothes. Please review the following criteria before submitting your items for collaboration:

Quality Standards

  • Condition: Clothes must be in excellent condition, free from stains, tears, and excessive wear.
  • Cleanliness: All items must be freshly laundered or dry-cleaned.
  • Functionality: Ensure all zippers, buttons, and fasteners are in working order.

Style and Trends

  • Current Trends: We are looking for pieces that align with current Gen Z fashion trends.
  • Unique Pieces: Vintage and one-of-a-kind items are highly encouraged.
  • Variety: We welcome a diverse range of styles, including casual, formal, and streetwear.

Sustainability

  • Eco-Friendly Fabrics: Preference will be given to items made from sustainable materials such as organic cotton, linen, and recycled fabrics.
  • Minimal Packaging: Please minimize the use of plastic and non-recyclable packaging when submitting clothes.

Submission Process

  • Contact Us: Reach out through our collaboration form or email us at aestheticize@gmail.com
  • Provide Details: Include photos and descriptions of the items you wish to submit.
  • Approval: Our team will review your submission and get back to you within 4-5 business days.
  • Shipping Instructions: Upon approval, we will provide shipping instructions or arrange for pick-up if you're local

Terms & Conditions:

  • All deals are final. Store credit cannot be converted to cash at a later date.
  • Slot fees are non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • Staff decisions are final. Please respect our expertise and judgment.
  • Store credit cannot be combined with in-store discounts or offers.
